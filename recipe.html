Fresh Garden Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

1 cup mixed greens (lettuce, arugula, spinach)
1/2 cucumber, sliced
1/2 bell pepper, chopped
1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup shredded carrots
1/4 cup sliced red onion
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste

Steps to Make

  1. Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Slice and chop the vegetables as listed.
  3. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper, tomatoes, carrots, and red onion.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Health Benefits

  • Rich in vitamins and minerals, providing a nutrient boost.
  • High in fiber, promoting good digestive health.
  • Low in calories, making it a great option for weight management.
  • Contains healthy fats from olive oil, beneficial for heart health.
